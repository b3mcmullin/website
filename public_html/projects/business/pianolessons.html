Beginning Piano Lessons

About

Welcome to this Beginning Piano section! I have taken piano lessons since 2011 and I currently participate annually in the NVMTA (Norther Virginia Music Teachers Association) theory test as well as Bach Baroque and Fall Festival. I started teaching piano in 2020 and I hope that the beginning students I teach will learn basic piano skills while having fun exploring on their own and creating their own music.


What I provide

  1. Beginner Piano Lessons
    • 15 or 30 minute lessons
    • Focus on basic piano skills as well as creation of the student's own pieces
    • Learning with games that teach concepts in a fun way
  2. Optional theory instruction Beyond what is taught in throught the music including basic...
    • Rhythm
    • Listening to identify notes
    • Articulation (identifying and learning about notations in music and how that translates to playing
    • Basic Scale and Music Writing

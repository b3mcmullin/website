About

I have multiple years of experience in lawn care, doing mowing, trimming and blowing as well as other yard and property work. I provide full lawn care including mowing, trimming and blowing. Along with general lawn treatment, I am also available to do mulching, seeding, weeding and chemical treatment for your outdoor areas. Additionally, I can do leaf cleanup and shovel/salt for ice/snow.
